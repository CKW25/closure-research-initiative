# Research Conduct

Discussion in this repository concerns the public research record of the Closure Research Initiative: definitions, hypotheses, proofs, citations, versions, deployment integrity, and discoverability.

Participants are expected to:

- address the statement, proof, citation, or implementation at issue;
- cite the relevant work, version, section, theorem or definition label, and page number where possible;
- distinguish proof errors, definitional ambiguities, missing hypotheses, citation problems, and disagreement with premises;
- avoid personal attacks, dismissive rhetoric, harassment, or bad-faith repetition;
- respect that public manuscript availability is for reading, citation, criticism, and review, not an open license grant.

Reports about conduct or security-sensitive matters may be sent to [chast.wolfe@closureresearchinitiative.org](mailto:chast.wolfe@closureresearchinitiative.org).

This policy is intended to preserve a serious technical forum. It does not restrict rigorous criticism of the work.
