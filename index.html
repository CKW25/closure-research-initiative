Research Program

The Closure Research Initiative studies what remains of physical theory when no external scaffolding is allowed to enter as primitive: no background manifold, no pre-supplied state space, no external observer, no gauge convention, no causal order, and no field content imposed in advance. The primitive condition is intrinsicality: a comparison is a relation among states, invariant under the symmetries it constitutes, not a label applied from outside.
Starting from primitive comparison data, the program derives a rigid architecture—quotient semantics, transport obstruction, curvature at the faithful smooth-realization stage—and establishes that a genuinely closed realized three-dimensional system is forced to the S³ case. No external geometric, dynamic, or quantum structure is assumed.
