Argues that the cosmological and structural uses of “open” and “closed” operate at different logical levels and cannot be identified. Structural closure is required for invariant physical content, not a geometric condition.

Scope: this landing-page summary is informal; the binding statements are the definitions, hypotheses, and theorems in the paper. Internal orientation: Overview for the structural-closure claim, Notation for terms, and Reading Guide for the linked general-relativity and cosmology background sources.
