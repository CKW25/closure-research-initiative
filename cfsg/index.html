At the manifold stage, a genuinely closed realized three-dimensional system is forced to the S³ case. The closure constraint, expressed through the orthonormal frame bundle and the admissible comparison groupoid, entails the rigidity result within that smooth-realization setting.

Scope: this landing-page summary is informal; the binding statements are the definitions, hypotheses, and theorem in the paper. The result is conditional on the faithful smooth-realization bridge; see Notation and Objections for that qualification, and Overview for the linked differential-geometric background sources.
