This page collects outside works cited by the Closure Research Initiative for background, comparison, or experimental context. Closure-specific theorem claims are sourced to the internal monograph and papers on Preprints; those internal works are not duplicated here.

The links below point to DOI, publisher, arXiv, archive, or institutional pages. They are citation links, not redistributed copies of copyrighted works.
