## Welcome to the Closure Research Initiative Discussions

This forum is for scholarly discussion of the closure program: its definitions, results, proofs, implications, and extensions.

### Purpose

These discussions serve as a formal complement to the preprints hosted at [closureresearchinitiative.org](https://closureresearchinitiative.org). They are intended for:

- Technical questions about specific definitions, lemmas, or theorems
- Clarification of the logical architecture and dependency structure
- Discussion of connections to existing literature in gauge theory, general relativity, and foundations of physics
- Proposals for extensions, alternative formulations, or applications
- Notification of errors, omissions, or required corrections

### Categories

| Category | When to Use |
|---|---|
| **Announcements** | New preprints, revisions, program updates |
| **Discussion** | Open-ended conversation about the closure program |
| **Proposals** | Suggestions for extensions, applications, or alternative formulations |
| **Questions & Answers** | Technical questions about specific results or definitions |
| **Polls** | Structured community input on program priorities |
| **Applications** | Applications of closure-theoretic methods to specific problems |

### Guidelines

1. **Cite specific passages.** When asking a question or raising a point, reference the relevant preprint, section, and page number where possible.

2. **Distinguish logical criticism from programmatic disagreement.** Logical errors in definitions or proofs are a distinct class from disagreement about premises or interpretation. Please mark which you intend.

3. **No ad hominem or dismissive rhetoric.** Engage with the mathematics, not the mathematician.

4. **Attribution.** All contributions are presumed attributable to the author's name. Anonymous or pseudonymous contributions will not be considered in formal errata.

5. **Corrections.** If you identify an error in any preprint, please open a discussion under Questions & Answers with "ERRATA:" in the title, citing the specific location and the proposed correction.

### Prior Work

All preprints are available at [closureresearchinitiative.org](https://closureresearchinitiative.org). The [Reading Guide](/guide/) provides recommended sequences through the papers.
